ABHAYA Panic Button

Instantly alert authorities and your emergency contacts with the panic button.

How to Use the Panic Button

  1. Ensure your device has internet access.
  2. Click the "Panic Button" to send an alert.
  3. Emergency contacts and authorities will be notified immediately.
  4. Stay in a safe location if possible while waiting for assistance.
  5. Follow any further instructions provided by the app or emergency services.
