ABHAYA Safety Tips & Resources

Empowering You with Essential Safety Knowledge

"Your safety is our top priority. Stay aware, stay prepared, and stay strong!"

1. Personal Safety

Always be vigilant of your surroundings. Trust your instincts and seek help if needed.

2. Emergency Contacts

Have a list of emergency numbers handy. Make sure they are updated and easily accessible.

3. Safe Communication

Use secure communication methods and be cautious about sharing personal information.

4. Travel Safety

Share your travel plans with someone you trust and avoid isolated or unfamiliar areas.

5. Self-Defense

Consider taking self-defense classes to build confidence and learn essential skills for protection.

6. Online Safety

Protect your personal information online and be cautious of phishing scams and fraud.

7. Cybersecurity

Use strong, unique passwords for each of your accounts and enable two-factor authentication where possible.

8. First Aid Knowledge

Learn basic first aid skills to handle minor injuries or medical emergencies until professional help arrives.

9. Home Safety

Ensure your home is secure with working locks and alarms. Keep emergency exits clear and accessible.

10. Community Awareness

Stay informed about local safety issues and participate in community safety programs and initiatives.
